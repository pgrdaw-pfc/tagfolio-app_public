Adding Images to Report

Select specific images to include in your report generation.

To add images:

  1. Select images in the gallery view.
  2. Click the "Add" button in the report toolbar.

Sorting Images in Report

Arrange the order of images within your report for better presentation.

To sort images:

  1. Drag and drop the image thumbnails to reorder them.
Back to top ↑

Saving Reports

Save your report configuration to regenerate it later or keep a record.

To save a report:

  1. Enter a name for the report and save.
  2. Choose a report type in the dropdown.
  3. Click the "Save" button in the report toolbar.
Back to top ↑

Sharing Reports

Share your generated reports with colleagues or clients.

To share a report:

  1. Generate or open a saved report.
  2. Click the "Share" button to copy its URL in the clipboard.
Back to top ↑

Copying Report Link

Get a direct link to your report to share easily.

To copy the link:

  1. Find your shared report in the sidebar.
  2. Click the "Share" button next to the report name to copy the URL in the clipboard.
Back to top ↑

Viewing Shared Reports

Anonymous and registered users can see shared reports.

To see a shared report:

  1. Copy the URL in the clipboard as explained before.
  2. Paste it in the browser.
  3. You will see the report type selected ready to be printed.
